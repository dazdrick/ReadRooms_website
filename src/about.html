Our story

We started in 2015, as modest collection of books and has grown into a modern learning center,
combining traditional learning methods with technologies

Mission

The ReadRooms is more than just a collection of books - it's a hub for learning and creativity.
Our mission to provide high-quality resources with collection of books and modern technologies.
This is the place where knowledge meets with innovation.

Provided services

We provide study space for your comfortable learning and achieving goals.
Beyond traditional books, you can have access to online databases with useful resources and interactive study tools.

We host events, workshops, technological and cultural conferences.
Please contact us for more details.
